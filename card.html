Clownfish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish's skin makes it immune to the fish-eating anemone's lethal sting.

  • Scientic Name: Amphiproninae
  • Average Length: 11 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 7 years
  • Habitat: Tropical coral reefs

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars, clown anemonefish are among the most recognizable of all reefdwellers. They reach about 11 centimeters in length, and are named for the mutlicolored sea anemone in which they make their homes.
